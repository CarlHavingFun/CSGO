# Sources and Rights Notes

## Primary roster source
- HLTV Counter-Strike World ranking on June 1st, 2026: https://www.hltv.org/ranking/teams/2026/june/1

## Scope notes
- Top 20 teams are taken from the HLTV ranking page.
- The HLTV ranking page lists 98 player names across Top 20 because MOUZ and FaZe show four players each on that snapshot. No fifth player was fabricated in `players.csv`.
- `team_periods.csv` includes a current-period record for every listed player. The first-pass expanded historical periods are filled for the 20 sample-priority characters and should be reviewed before legal/public release.

## Identity and IP safety
- Characters are original fictionalized personas. They borrow region, role, and playstyle archetype only.
- Prompts explicitly forbid copying real player faces or presenting generated characters as the real person.
- The selected jersey policy is "authentic real team jersey". This can include team colors, logos, sponsor layouts, and era-specific apparel design. This is suitable for internal visual exploration and reference boards, but carries trademark/sponsor/right-of-publicity risk for public monetized release.
- Recommended public-release pass: generate a second wardrobe set with fictionalized team names, modified logos, and sponsor-free jersey layouts.
